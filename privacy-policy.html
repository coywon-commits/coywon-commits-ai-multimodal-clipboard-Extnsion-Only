Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Clipboard ("the Extension"), developed by Coywon, handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Overview

AI Clipboard is a Chrome browser extension that automatically detects and copies AI-generated responses from supported AI platforms (such as ChatGPT and Claude) to your clipboard. The Extension is designed to operate entirely on your local device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, age, etc.)
  • Health or financial information
  • Authentication credentials
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • The content of AI responses you copy

3. How the Extension Works

When you activate the Extension on a supported AI platform page, a content script reads the AI-generated response text directly from the page's DOM and writes it to your system clipboard using the clipboardWrite API. This process happens entirely on your device. No data is sent to any external server operated by us.

4. Permissions Used

  • activeTab – Accesses the currently active tab only when you click the extension button.
  • scripting – Injects a content script to read AI response text from the page DOM.
  • storage – Saves your local preferences (e.g., enabled/disabled toggle) on your device only.
  • clipboardWrite – Writes the extracted AI response to your clipboard.
  • Host permissions – Limited to supported AI platform domains (e.g., chat.openai.com, claude.ai) necessary for the Extension's core function.

5. Local Storage

The Extension may store user preferences (such as whether auto-copy is enabled) using Chrome's local storage API. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. It does not sell or transfer user data to any third party.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
